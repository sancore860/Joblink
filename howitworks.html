How It Works ?

Simple steps to connect job seekers and employers

For Job Seekers

1. Sign Up

Join JobLink by creating a free account.

Fill out your profile with details like your skills, preferred job types, availability, and current location. This helps us match you with the most suitable opportunities nearby.

Register Now

2. Browse Jobs

After logging in, access the job dashboard to explore a list of daily and short-term jobs. Use filters to narrow results by job type, pay range, location, or required skills. Get real-time updates as new jobs are posted.

Login to Browse

3. Apply Instantly

Click the "Apply" button on any job post you're interested in. No need for long applications—employers will review your profile and get in touch. Get notifications when you're selected or messaged by an employer.

Login to Apply

4. Start Working

Once selected, you'll receive instructions on the job location, time, and contact person. Complete the task and get paid as agreed. You can also rate the employer and add completed jobs to your experience.

For Employee Seekers

1. Post a Job

Create a job post by entering details such as job title, description, required skills, location, date/time, and payment amount. Posting is quick, and your job goes live instantly to workers in your area.

Post a Job

2. Get Matched

Our smart system will show your job to the most relevant job seekers based on skills and location. You'll start receiving interest from potential workers quickly, often within minutes of posting.

3. Hire & Connect

Review applicants, check their profiles and ratings, and choose the right person. Use in-app messaging to communicate job details. Once confirmed, the worker starts and you can track the job to completion.

Login to Hire
